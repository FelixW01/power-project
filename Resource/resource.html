Hot Line

National Domestic Violence Hotline - They offer support in multiple languages. You can reach them at 1-800-799-SAFE (7233) and ask for language assistance.

SAMHSA National Helpline - For substance abuse and mental health support, you can call 1-800-662-HELP (4357). They provide referrals and can connect you with multilingual services.
